Welcome to myReads!

Abstract

Welcome to myReads! To get started, click "Search LOC" above and starting searching the stacks! And don't worry, the search is restricted to digitized materials only, so you don't even have to go to the library to pull your first text.

Book List

Create a list of books! This list is saved to your local storage, so you won't have to worry about losing your results when you close your browser window. Just be sure not to clear that cache!

Search the LOC

This tool allows you to query the books catalog of the Library of Congress. What is unique about this database is that it contains more than just books. It contains many different types of print materials that can include scans of portraits, court decisions, and much more!
